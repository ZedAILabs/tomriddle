Welcome to Tom Riddle

Navigate through the windows to unlock exclusive insights into Tom's sound, limited-edition merch, and future shows. The experience is yours to explore.

Brisbane producer Tom Riddle has quickly established himself as one of Queensland's rising talents.

Starting young, he made his festival debut at just 16. In 2020, Tom released a series of club-ready tracks that captured attention and kept the energy high across all platforms.

His sound is defined by deep, driving bass lines, intricate highs, and sharp synths, blending together to create a distinctive style that evolves with every release.
